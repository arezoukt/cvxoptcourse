  • Lectures: pre-recorded lectures uploaded weekly. There will be weekly live discussion sessions where Dr. Keshavarz will work through examples and answer questions. These will be held on Google Meet, Wednesdays 2:30-3:30pm.

  • TA office hours/tutorials: Mohammad (XX), Sajad (XX). These will also be held on Google Meet.

Prerequisites

  • Good knowledge of linear algebra, and exposure to probability. Exposure to numerical computing, optimization, and application fields helpful but not required; the applications will be kept basic and simple.

  • You will use one of CVX (Matlab) or CVXPY (Python) to write simple scripts, so basic familiarity with elementary programming will be required. We refer to CVX, CVXPY, CVXR, and Convex.jl collectively as CVX*.
