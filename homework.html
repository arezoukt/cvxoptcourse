Homework

Throughout the course we will have bi-Weekly homework assignments, due on Fridays at midnight. This year we will be using quera for homework submissions, as well as holding online discussions and peer to peer interactions. The enrollment code for Quera is cvxopt1400.

Late homework will not be accepted, except in family or medical emergency and must be approved by the instructor directly ahead of time. You are allowed, even encouraged, to work on the homework in small groups, but you must write up your own homework to hand in. Homework will be graded on a scale of {0, 1, 2}. We will have 8-10 homework assignments throughout the term and will consider the average grade of these homeworks towards the total grade.
